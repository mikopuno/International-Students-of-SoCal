Who We Are

Southern California International Student Support is a community-driven platform dedicated to helping international students navigate mental health challenges while studying abroad.

Our Vision

We strive to create a welcoming environment where international students can connect, share experiences, and find the resources they need to thrive academically, socially, and emotionally.

Why This Matters

Studying in a foreign country can be an exciting yet overwhelming experience. Language barriers, cultural adjustments, and social isolation can take a toll on mental well-being. Our goal is to break the stigma around mental health and provide the necessary support for international students in Southern California.
